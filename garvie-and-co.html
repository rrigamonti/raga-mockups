Garvie & Co

Garvie and Co sits at the heart of Milngavie, at the iconic starting point of the West Highland Way. Our restaurant serves seasonal Scottish dishes, our bar pours craft drinks, and our bakery creates artisan bread and pastries daily.

Whether you are fuelling up before a hike or settling in for a leisurely dinner, Garvie and Co is your neighbourhood gathering place.
